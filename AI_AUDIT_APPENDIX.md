Potential risks of AI use include:

1. Over-confident wording without sufficient statistical nuance.
2. Documentation quality that may appear complete even if code is not executed.
3. Possible mismatch between plain-language explanations and formal econometric detail.

Mitigations applied:

1. Re-executed notebook cells to confirm outputs.
2. Cross-checked required figures and comparison outputs.
3. Kept interpretation statements tied to observable model behavior.
